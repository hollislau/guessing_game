Game Information

History

Rock Paper Scissors Lizard Spock, created by Sam Kass and Karen Bryla, is an expanded version of the classic Rock Paper Scissors game.
This expanded version was made famous by the characters in the show, The Big Bang Theory.

Game Rules

The computer opponent, Sheldon, challenges the player to a game of Rock Paper Scissors Lizard Spock.
The player is asked for a name and is then prompted to select from the five available choices: rock, paper, scissors, lizard, or Spock.
A random selection is made for Sheldon. Based on the selections, a winner for the match is determined as follows:

  • Scissors cuts paper
  • Paper covers rock
  • Rock crushes lizard
  • Lizard poisons Spock
  • Spock smashes scissors
  • Scissors decapitates lizard
  • Lizard eats paper
  • Paper disproves Spock
  • Spock vaporizes rock
  • (and as it always has) Rock crushes scissors

Following the match, the player may play again as often as desired, and the number of matches and victories earned by Sheldon and the player are tracked.
